About Me

My name is Fabio Campioni and I am an M.Sc student in the School of Computing at Queen's University! I am originally from Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario. I received my Bachelor of Computer Science (Honours) from Algoma University in 2013. My research interests include algorithm design and networking. My M.Sc thesis involves the design of a heuristic recruitment algorithm for vehicular participants in crowd sensing under the supervision of Dr. Salimur Choudhury, Dr. Kai Salomaa, and Dr. Selim Akl.
